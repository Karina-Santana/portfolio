About

I'm Karina

In 2018 I made a decision to change my career and move from Architecture to something that satisfied me professionally.

While I was figuring out what I wanted to do I had a great opportunity to work in a Czech marketplace where I had contact with the Product and IT departments which my curiosity about the tech world only increased.

In January 2022 I moved to Sydney already knowing what I wanted to do.
I researched the best Software Engineering courses in Sydney and found General Assembly's immersive course. It was three months of intensive course, challenging myself and enjoying the field more every day.

What I like the most is the fact that I can use the best parts of Architecture as solving problems, building and designing beautiful things, such as software and interactive interfaces.

My main professional goal is to find a company where I can learn, grow and feel that I’m impacting somehow in people's lives. If you want to know more, feel free to message me - I’d love to chat!
