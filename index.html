A little about my journey

I am Brazilian living in Sydney and recently graduated in Software Engineering from General Assembly.

In 2018 I made a decision to change my career and move from Architecture to something that satisfied me professionally.

While I was figuring out what I wanted to do I had a great opportunity to work in a Czech marketplace where I had contact with the Product and IT departments which my curiosity about the tech world only increased.

In January 2022 I moved to Sydney already knowing what I wanted to do.
I researched the best Software Engineering courses in Sydney and found General Assembly's immersive course quite interesting. I enrolled it and it was three months of intensive course, challenging myself and enjoying the field more every day.

Now my main professional goal is to find a company where I can learn, grow and feel that I’m impacting somehow in people's lives. If you want to know more, feel free to message me - I’d love to chat!
